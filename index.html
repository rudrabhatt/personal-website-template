Hi, I'm Rudra I'm a 4th year Management Engineering student from the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about changing the world through innovative technologies, and working on problems that have never been solved before. I have worked for the consumer electronics, automotive, and insurance industry. Now, I am ready to take on a full-time challenge that allows me to manage projects by working extensively with design engineering teams!

Work Experience

 I worked at Apple as an iPhone Engineering Program Manager. I had the opportunity to work with Electrical Engineers, Product Designers, Operations Project Managers, and Safety/Quality Engineers.
Tesla
I worked at Tesla as a Powertrain NPI Program Manager. Specifically, I focused on the small and large drive units for Model S and Model X. I worked with a wide range of teams to successfully introduce new part/product changes onto the manufacturing line!
Fairfax
I worked for 3 companies under the Fairfax umbrella. I worked at Northbridge Insurance as a Business Analyst, and I introduced a new technology into the Claims sector. I worked at FairVentures, which is a start-up in Communitech, where I wrote a business document for how to introduce a new technology. Finally, I worked at Zenith Insurance, where I worked on an agile project, and helped the team save a lot of time!
Toyota
I worked at Toyota as a Business Analyst, where I collected requirements from internal teams to build a company SharePoint Intranet. I translated these requirements into technical tasks for developers, and worked with designers to create beautiful pages!
